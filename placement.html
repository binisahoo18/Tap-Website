Placements At Tap Academy

At TapAcademy, we understand how important it is for freshers to have a quality
start to their careers with meaningful job opportunities in their choice of
industry & for working professionals to get lateral job opportunities. Hence, our
team works out detailed mechanisms that allow us to serve our students with
comprehensive placement support & help them get closer to their dream.

Our dedicated placement team is constantly striving to build a successful &
rewarding career for our students. From the very first day, they walk through
the doors of our institute, we make them industry-ready with comprehensive
training & practical expertise.

Placement Cell

Step 1: Counselling & Admission

Students take admission in one of our courses after going through detailed counseling sessions with our counseling team.

Step 2: Soft Skills

Soft Skills Development is now a very integral part of our training as it allows us to train students to progress quicker in their corporate careers & helps give them an edge in their future jobs/workplaces.

Step 3: Mock Interviews

Even after you know it all, Interviews aren't easy to crack. Our placements team conducts a series of mock interviews to help prepare candidates for the tough questions & make them ready for what will be their final hurdle.

Step 4: Classes & Training

Students undergo their classes which are filled with real-life examples & industry-ready curriculum that details the technical know-how of their industry to them.

Step 5: CV & Linkedin Profile Building

CVs & Linkedin are the first things employers check if they are interested in hiring you & we help you build profiles that help in creating strong impressions on recruiters.

Step 6: Final Selection

Our placements team works on the final stages of your application & helps fix a series of interviews for you in organizations where your preferred position is available as well as with our placement partners.
